Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our reseller program

How quickly can I start selling after purchasing a plan?

You can start selling immediately after purchase. Once your payment is confirmed, you'll receive instant access to your reseller panel and can begin generating keys right away.

What kind of support do resellers receive?

Our resellers receive priority 24/7 support through our dedicated Discord channel. You'll get faster response times and specialized assistance for any issues related to your reseller business.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Contact our support team, and they'll help you transition to a higher-tier plan with the appropriate pricing adjustments.

Is there a limit to how many keys I can generate?

No, there's no limit. Our reseller plans allow you to generate unlimited keys for the products included in your plan.

How do I receive updates for the products?

All product updates are automatically available in your reseller panel. You'll also receive notifications in our Discord server whenever new updates are released.
